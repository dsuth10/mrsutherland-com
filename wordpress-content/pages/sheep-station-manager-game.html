About This Game

Sheep Station Manager is an educational math game designed for Year 5 students. Players learn about:

  • Multiplication and unit rates
  • Profit and loss calculations
  • Seasonal market fluctuations
  • Financial planning and decision-making

The game simulates running a sheep farm where students must purchase sheep, manage housing, calculate feed costs, and determine wool income to make a profit.
